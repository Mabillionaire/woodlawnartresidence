OUR MISSION

Artists Stay Where They Are Valued

The Woodlawn Residence for Artists provides quality affordable housing for artists to connect and grow with each other and their community. We cultivate creative environments where artists can thrive while contributing to neighborhood cultural vitality and resisting displacement.

We recognize that artists often create vibrant communities that then become unaffordable to them. Our model inverts this approach by intentionally creating affordable infrastructure that sustains creative energy.
